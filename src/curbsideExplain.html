Introducing Hassle-Free Curbside Pickup


At Toy Store, we understand that convenience is key. That's why we are thrilled to announce our new curbside pickup service, designed with you in mind. With our curbside pickup service, we bring the joy of toy shopping right to your car.


How to start your first curbside pickup order:

  • Explore our hundreds of products and seamlessly add your desired items to your shopping cart
  • Once you have completed your shopping, navigate to your cart and click the "checkout" option
  • On the Checkout Screen, select your preferred pickup time, and proceed to complete your payment, confirming your order
  • Upon your arrival at the store, conveniently park in the designated curbside pickup spaces, and enter the number on the space into the website
  • Relax in the comfort of your car as our dedicated team brings your order directly to you, ensuring that its safely placed in your vehicle.

Join us as we make toy shopping easier, faster, and more enchanting than ever before!
