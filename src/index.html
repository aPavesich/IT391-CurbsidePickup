About Us

Welcome to Toy Store, where imagination knows no bounds and joy knows no age. We are your one-stop destination for all things play, a haven for children and the young at heart. Nestled in the heart of Normal, IL, we've been spreading happiness through toys since 2023.

Our Mission

At Toy Store, our mission is to bring smiles and foster imagination. We believe that play is an essential part of childhood, one that shapes young minds and creates lasting memories. We strive to provide a wide range of toys and games that encourage learning, social interaction, and, most importantly, endless fun.

Our Story

Toy Store was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to create a magical place where kids and families could explore, ream, and connect through the wonder of toys. Our journey began when a group of passionate toy enthusiasts came together to curate an exceptional collection of toys, games, and playtime essentials. We believe in the enduring power of play to inspire, educate, and spark creativity in children of all ages.
